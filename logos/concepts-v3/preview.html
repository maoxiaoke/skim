Skim — Logo Concepts v3

New directions — six distinct pattern families, all on the teal→blue brand gradient. Each shown at icon size + favicon size.
A · Stacked cards — geometric / layered
A fanned stack of skill cards — you skim through a managed collection. Friendly, "library of things" reading; front card is the active one.
B · Skill grid — dot matrix
A 4×4 library with one skill selected/active. Modular, app-launcher feel; clearly says "manager of many skills."
C · S ribbon — letter monogram ★
A single-stroke geometric "S". Most ownable and on-name; scales to a favicon better than any other here.
D · Skim lines — line system / negative space ★
Content lines cut out of a panel — the literal act of skim-reading. Bold, confident, instantly legible at any size.
E · Capability hub — node network
One manager orchestrating many skills, radiating asymmetrically. Strong "system/connector" story; slightly more generic.
F · Skip ripple — concentric rings + dot
A skimmed stone and the ripples it leaves — a literal, poetic take on the name. Distinct silhouette; reads as "origin/spread."
